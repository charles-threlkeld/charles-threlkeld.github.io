8. Conclusion

8.1. We want to create a set of speech acts that is pragmatic, apart from syntax and (particularly) semantics

8.2. The schema should differentiate based on conversational context elements, like speaker switch

8.3. The schema should work to show how utterances link together (adjacency pairs)

8.4. Truth values and explanatory labels are secondary; they simply help us understand the labels we derive

8.5. Our schema is derived from conversational data, rather than task-based ad hoc or verified post hoc .
